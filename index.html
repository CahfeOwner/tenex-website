About

We work with founders who want more from their business

Tenex Business Performance is a management consultancy for UK SME founders. We work with businesses that have outgrown the early stages and need to run better — whether that means cleaner operations, stronger governance, smarter use of data or simply more time back for the people running them.

Our approach is straightforward: understand the business first, then act. We identify what is actually holding performance back before reaching for any solution — including AI. When technology features in our work, it is because it genuinely moves the needle, not because it is the current topic of conversation.

Behind Tenex is David McWhir — over 38 years in UK regulated industries spanning financial services, hospitality, care and manufacturing. That breadth of experience means we see patterns across sectors that specialists miss, and we bring the credibility to have the difficult conversations that drive real change.
