About

We work with ambitious organisations at inflection points

Tenex Business Performance is a management consultancy working across two distinct client types: founder-led SMEs that have outgrown the early stages and need to run better, and established financial services businesses navigating strategic change — whether that means AI adoption, operational transformation, regulatory pressure or building toward a transaction.

Our approach is straightforward: understand the business first, then act. We identify what is actually holding performance back before reaching for any solution — including AI. When AI features in our work, it is embedded into how the business actually operates, and the capability it builds stays with the business, not with us.

The AI challenge

Most organisations are not yet built to capture the value of AI

The AI narrative has shifted. The question is no longer whether AI will change your business. It already has. The question every organisation now faces is whether it can actually use the capability available to it.

Microsoft's 2026 Work Trend Index found that only 19% of AI users sit in what Microsoft calls the "Frontier" zone — where individual capability and organisational readiness genuinely reinforce each other. For the other 81%, AI investment is underperforming. Organisational factors account for more than twice the impact of individual mindset and behaviour. The tools are not the bottleneck. The organisation is.

19% of AI users are in the "Frontier" zone — where individual capability and organisational readiness genuinely reinforce each other. Most are not there yet.

01

Buy and hope

Tools are purchased, licences issued. Nothing changes, because nobody has redesigned how the work actually gets done. The technology is there. The organisation is not ready for it.

02

Delegate and disconnect

The AI mandate goes to one enthusiast or an isolated team. They have the tools. What they lack is the authority to change how the rest of the organisation works. The capability stays contained.

03

Outsource and exit

A consultancy delivers a strategy, a framework, a set of recommendations. The knowledge stays with them. When the engagement ends, so does the progress. The business is no more capable than before.

Tenex takes a different approach. We embed alongside your team, build the infrastructure that makes AI work in your specific business, and leave the capability inside the organisation. Not bolted on. Not delegated. Not outsourced. Yours.
