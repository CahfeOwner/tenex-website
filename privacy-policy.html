3. How we use your data

We use the information you provide solely to respond to your enquiry. We will not use it to send you marketing communications without your explicit consent, and we will not sell, rent or share it with third parties for their own purposes.

4. Lawful basis for processing

Our lawful basis for processing your contact form data is legitimate interests — specifically, our legitimate interest in responding to business enquiries submitted to us. We have assessed that this does not override your rights and interests as an individual.

5. How long we keep your data

We retain contact form submissions and related correspondence for up to two years from the date of last contact, unless a longer retention period is required by law or by the nature of any subsequent engagement.

6. Third-party processors

Contact form submissions are processed by Formspree (formspree.io), a third-party form handling service. Formspree processes data on our behalf and is subject to its own privacy policy. We do not store form submissions on any other external platform.

7. Your rights

Under UK GDPR, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Request restriction of processing
  • Data portability (where applicable)

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at info@tenexbp.co.uk. We will respond within one month.

8. Complaints

If you believe we have not handled your personal data correctly, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO):

  • Website: ico.org.uk

We would always prefer the opportunity to address any concern directly before you contact the ICO — please reach out to us first.
